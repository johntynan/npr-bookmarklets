NPR Bookmarklets

To add any of the following bookmarklets to your browser, just drag the any (or all) of the links below into your browser's bookmarks bar. (In Internet Explorer, please right-click on the link and choose "Add to favorites.")

  1. Add Story to List
  2. Get Transcript
  3. Text as Timeline
  4. Related Stories

What is a bookmarklet?

The links above are not plain HTML links; they're small bits of JavaScript code that you add to your browser's bookmarks or favorites bar. These act like mini-utilities which allow you to perform various searches of content at NPR.org based on selected text, get a transcript of a story that you're reading NPR.org, and more.
